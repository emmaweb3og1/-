Why Static Sites Rock

Published: July 30, 2025

Static sites are fast, secure, and easy to deploy. You don’t need a server-side runtime—just HTML, CSS, and a hosting service. That simplicity often translates to fewer headaches and blazing load times.

Next time you need a blog or a portfolio, consider going static. Your readers (and your future self) will thank you.
